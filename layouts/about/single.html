Why this exists

AI List exists to make the fast-moving AI industry easier to map. The goal is a neutral, structured, and open directory that helps builders, researchers, buyers, and curious people compare products without vendor spin.

The project tracks practical details like pricing models, platform support, open-source status, founding years, developer features, compliance signals, and the categories each product belongs to.

The data is open. The site is open source. Corrections, additions, category improvements, and new AI product submissions are welcome via GitHub.
