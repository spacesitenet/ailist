AI List is a curated open dataset of AI products, tools, platforms, models, and infrastructure. It provides structured data including pricing, supported platforms, headquarters, open-source status, and more.

## Key Pages

- [Product Directory]({{ .Site.BaseURL }}products/): Filterable table of all products
- [Categories]({{ .Site.BaseURL }}categories/): Products grouped by type and AI use case
- [Platforms]({{ .Site.BaseURL }}platforms/): Products by supported platform
- [Full LLM Context]({{ .Site.BaseURL }}llms-full.txt): Complete product data in text format

## Product Data Schema

Each product includes: name, description, website, year_founded, headquarters, open_source (boolean), self_signup (boolean), discontinued (boolean), categories, platforms, pricing models (delivery type, plans with monthly/yearly pricing), screen statistics, and more.

## Categories

- AI apps: End-user products powered by AI
- Developer tools: APIs, SDKs, agents, and infrastructure for builders
- Models and research: Model providers, labs, benchmarks, and model tooling
